    • - Can I add my own concoction?

      Yes! Train your memory is about adding your uniqueness and remembering who you are, as well as training yourself to keep a better eye on your spending ;)

    • - Can I have more than one drink?

      Yes! Keep track of all the drinks you've had and continue to have throughout the night!

    • - Who can see my drinks?

      Only your friends! Your privacy is important to us, we don't need to know, you need to know! To Train!

    • - Is there a limit?

  • Locations

    • - Can anyone see my location?

      Nope! Literally no one.

    • - Can I have drinks at the same location over a period of time?

      Yes! Train your memory allows you to analyse and learn your drinking habits, good or bad. You can log drinks at the same location, 2 weeks apart.

    • - What locations are available?

      Whatever you want to enter! Your mom's house, bff or S/O, all yours to choose from!

    • - What is my history?

      The history is the past occurences of a drink, location and price that you logged on the application, showing the last most recent
